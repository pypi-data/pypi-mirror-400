Resize a dataset Explore subset

Upload any CSV that feels too large to work with. Choose the percentage you want to keep, and the resizer will return a smaller sample that preserves the overall feel of the data.

This is independent of the datasets listed in the ML Lab sidebar. You can bring in any CSV here.

We will sample roughly this share of rows. For very large files, even 5–10 % can be enough to explore patterns.

▸ Show advanced options

If you supply a valid column name (for example a target label), the resizer will allocate rows per class so proportions remain close to the original.
