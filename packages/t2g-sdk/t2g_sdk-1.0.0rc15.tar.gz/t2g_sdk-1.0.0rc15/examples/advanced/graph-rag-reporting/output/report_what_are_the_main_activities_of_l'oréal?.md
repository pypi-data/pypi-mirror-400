thought)# L'Oréal Main Activities and Performance Report (Fiscal Year 2024)

L'Oréal is a global leader in the cosmetics and beauty industry, characterized by a diversified portfolio of brands, a strong commitment to innovation, and a significant global presence. Its activities are organized across four primary divisions and supported by extensive R&D and sustainability initiatives.

## 1. Core Business Divisions
L'Oréal operates through four specialized divisions, each targeting specific market segments. In 2024, the **Beauté Dermatologique** division showed the highest growth rate.

| Division | Focus Area | Growth Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Beauté Dermatologique** | Clinical and dermatological skincare | 9.8% |
| **Produits Grand Public** | Mass-market consumer goods (e.g., L'Oréal Paris) | 5.4% |
| **Produits Professionnels** | Professional salon products and services | 5.3% |
| **Luxe** | High-end, luxury beauty brands (e.g., Lancôme) | 2.7% |

## 2. Brand Portfolio and Recent Acquisitions
The group manages a portfolio of **37 brands**. L'Oréal actively expands its market reach through strategic acquisitions and brand management.
*   **Key Brands:** Includes iconic names like L'Oréal Paris and Lancôme.
*   **Recent Strategic Acquisitions/Partnerships:** The group has recently integrated or invested in brands such as **Amouage**, **Jacquemus**, **Miu Miu**, **Dr.G**, and **Galderma**.

## 3. Global Market Presence
L'Oréal maintains a balanced geographic footprint, with Europe and North America remaining its largest markets by revenue share.

## 4. Innovation and Digital Transformation
Research and technology are central to L'Oréal's activities, focusing on "Beauty Tech" and advanced skincare science.
*   **Research & Development:** Filed **694 patents** in 2024 and operates specialized labs like **CreAItech**.
*   **Technological Platforms:** Developed **Skin Technology by L’Oréal** and the **Beauty Genius** AI tool.
*   **E-commerce:** Digital sales are a major pillar, generating **12.3 billion euros** in revenue.

## 5. Operational and Sustainability Performance
The company integrates social and environmental responsibility into its core operations.
*   **Financial Health:** Reported a revenue of **€43.48 billion**, an operating margin of **20%**, and a comparable growth of **5.1%**.
*   **Sustainability:** Achieved a **CDP Score of AAA**. Current environmental metrics include a **97% renewable energy rate**, **53% water recycling rate**, and **49% sustainable packaging**.
*   **Social Impact:** Managed the **Fonds L’Oréal pour les Femmes** with a fund allocation of **€70 million**.
*   **Human Capital:** Employs **90,000 people** with a high employee engagement rate of **79%**.
